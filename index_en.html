Performance Dashboard

E-Commerce

TABLEAU

An interactive Sales and Profit Performance dashboard providing a comprehensive overview of an e-commerce shop's performance. It presents crucial insights on sales and profit trends over time, regional performance, top customers, and top-selling products.

The dashboard prominently displays key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total sales, total profit, quantity ordered, and number of orders. These metrics offer a quick snapshot of the overall business performance and provide a foundation for further analysis.

By analyzing sales and profit trends over time, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify patterns and seasonality in customer demand. This information can drive strategic decision-making, such as adjusting inventory levels, planning promotions during peak seasons, or optimizing pricing strategies.

The breakdown of sales and profit by state and region highlights geographic areas that contribute the most to the company's success. It allows for a deeper understanding of regional preferences, customer behavior, and potential opportunities for market expansion or targeted marketing campaigns.

Identifying the top 5 customers by profit provides insights into the most valuable customer relationships. This information can guide efforts to nurture and retain these high-value customers, tailor marketing strategies to their needs, and explore opportunities for cross-selling or upselling.

Similarly, knowing the top 5 products by profit helps identify the best-performing items in the product portfolio. This knowledge can inform inventory management decisions, supply chain optimization, and product development strategies.

The histogram showcasing quantity ordered per customer provides visibility into purchasing patterns and customer loyalty. It can help identify frequent buyers, potential brand advocates, and opportunities for personalized marketing or loyalty programs.

Lastly, analyzing the average days to ship sheds light on the efficiency and effectiveness of order fulfillment processes. It enables the identification of bottlenecks or areas for improvement, ensuring timely delivery and customer satisfaction.

Overall, the Sales and Profit Performance dashboard empowers stakeholders to make data-driven decisions by providing actionable insights into sales and profit trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and operational efficiency. It serves as a valuable tool for driving growth, optimizing operations, and enhancing customer satisfaction in the dynamic e-commerce landscape.

TABLEAU - story

Key performance indicators (KPIs) and charts highlight important metrics such as revenue, COGS to Revenue ratio, and inventory turnover. Reporting Dashboard

Bike Rentals

TABLEAU

Rental activity based on different factors such as bike type, customer type, stations, hour of day, and day of week are presented through various interactive charts as well as key performance indicators (KPIs). Through this analysis, several valuable insights can be derived:

1. Rental Trends by Bike Type: The dashboard allows for a comparison of rental patterns across different bike types. This information helps identify which bike types are more popular among customers, enabling the company to optimize its bike inventory and cater to customer preferences effectively.

2. Customer Segmentation: By analyzing rentals based on customer type, the dashboard provides insights into the company's customer base. It helps identify whether the majority of customers are regular subscribers or occasional riders, allowing the company to tailor its marketing strategies and services accordingly.

3. Station Performance: The dashboard offers an overview of rental activities at different stations. This analysis helps identify high-demand stations and those that may require improvements in terms of bike availability or infrastructure. Understanding station performance enables the company to optimize resource allocation and enhance customer experience.

4. Hourly and Weekly Patterns: The dashboard presents rental trends by the hour of the day and day of the week. This information allows the company to identify peak hours and days when rentals are highest, helping optimize staffing levels, bike maintenance schedules, and operational planning.

The insights derived from the dashboard provide a comprehensive understanding of the business. This information empowers the company to make data-driven decisions, improve operational efficiency, enhance customer satisfaction, and maximize business growth.
